This Privacy Policy describes how Dutta Integrated Services (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects the information provided by users (“you”) of the RIDE_DIS mobile application, which is part of our internal logistics and order management system.

1. Purpose of the App

RIDE_DIS is intended for authorized drivers and delivery personnel only. The app facilitates access to delivery assignments, pickup and rescheduling requests, and order status updates.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information to manage logistics operations:

  • Driver's name and login credentials
  • Pickup and delivery assignment details
  • Order IDs and status updates (e.g., Pending, On Route, Delivered, Cancelled)
  • Timestamps and location-based updates (if location tracking is used)

3. Use of Information

The data collected is used exclusively to manage delivery tasks, track order progress, assign jobs, and improve operational efficiency. Data may also be used for auditing and customer service purposes.

4. Data Sharing and Access

No personal information is shared with external third parties. Collected data is accessible only to authorized personnel within Dutta Integrated Services for logistics coordination and monitoring.

5. Security Measures

We implement appropriate security practices to protect all user data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Access to the RIDE_DIS app is restricted to verified users only.

6. Location Data (if applicable)

If the app uses location services for route tracking or delivery verification, this data is used strictly for internal purposes and is not stored longer than necessary.
