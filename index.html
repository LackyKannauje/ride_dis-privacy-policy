This Privacy Policy outlines how Dutta Integrated Services (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information via the RIDE_DIS mobile application, a tool designed for authorized drivers within our logistics and CRM ecosystem.

1. Purpose of the App

RIDE_DIS is used by our delivery personnel to manage pickup and delivery tasks, update order statuses, and in some cases, create new service orders on behalf of customers.

2. Information We Collect

To support order processing and route management, the app may collect the following data:

  • Driver’s name and login credentials
  • Pickup and delivery order details
  • Order status updates (e.g., Pending, On Route, Delivered, Cancelled)
  • Customer information collected via order form:
    • Full Name
    • Phone Number (if provided)
    • Address or location details
  • Timestamps and optional location data (if tracking is enabled)

3. How We Use This Information

All data collected is used strictly for operational purposes, including creating and fulfilling customer orders, updating delivery progress, and ensuring timely service. Customer details entered by drivers are used solely to facilitate the requested service.

4. Data Sharing and Access

Information is not sold, rented, or shared with any third-party advertisers. Data is accessible only to authorized company personnel and is used within the scope of our internal CRM and order management system.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect all user and customer data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. Access to RIDE_DIS is restricted to verified employees or agents of Dutta Integrated Services.

6. Location Data (if applicable)

If enabled, location services may be used to support delivery tracking or proof-of-service. This data is processed securely and not retained longer than necessary.

7. Responsibility of Drivers

Drivers are responsible for collecting accurate customer information and are instructed to use the data only for legitimate order creation and delivery purposes within the app.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this policy periodically. Continued use of the application implies acceptance of the latest version.
